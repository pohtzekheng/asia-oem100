About the Programme

Asia is a global manufacturing hub for cosmetics, skincare, nutraceuticals, personal care and medical wellness — yet there has been no independent regional programme dedicated to the OEM, ODM and contract manufacturers behind these products.

The Asia Consumer Health & Beauty OEM 100™ closes that gap. It gives global brands a trusted, independent reference for selecting manufacturing partners, and gives outstanding manufacturers the recognition they deserve. The inaugural Top 100 will be announced on 10 July 2026.

Eligibility

  • Operate manufacturing facilities in Asia
  • Provide OEM, ODM or Contract Manufacturing services
  • At least three years of operation
  • Hold all required manufacturing licences in your jurisdiction
  • Agree to independent verification
